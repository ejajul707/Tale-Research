Our Publication

Seizure Detection

Our pioneering seizure detection algorithm combines multi-modal EEG signal processing with machine learning to predict seizure onset up to 15 minutes before clinical manifestation. This early warning system allows patients and caregivers crucial preparation time, reducing injury risk and improving quality of life for epilepsy patients.

VR + EEG Integration for Stroop Test

We've revolutionized cognitive assessment by integrating virtual reality with real-time EEG monitoring during modified Stroop tests. This immersive environment provides more ecologically valid testing conditions while capturing neural responses with millisecond precision, offering deeper insights into executive function, attention allocation, and cognitive interference.

Neuro Finance

Our Neuro Finance research bridges neuroscience and behavioral economics to decode the neural mechanisms underlying financial decision-making. By monitoring brain activity during simulated trading scenarios, we've identified neural markers that predict risk assessment, loss aversion, and decision confidence with remarkable accuracy.

Neuroimaging Analysis

Advanced processing of fMRI, EEG, and MEG data with proprietary algorithms that enhance signal detection and spatial resolution. Our techniques reveal neural activity patterns with unprecedented clarity.
